AI Trust Center

The AI Trust Center serves as your organization's comprehensive platform for demonstrating responsible AI practices, building stakeholder trust, and ensuring compliance with AI governance frameworks. This centralized system allows you to showcase your commitment to ethical AI, transparent data practices, and regulatory compliance through a professional, publicly accessible trust center.

System Overview

The AI Trust Center provides a complete solution for AI governance transparency, from content creation and management to public presentation. It helps organizations establish credibility, meet compliance requirements, and build trust with customers, partners, and regulators through a structured, professional trust center that can be shared publicly.

AI Trust Center Components

Overview Tab

The Overview tab serves as the foundation of your AI Trust Center, containing essential information about your organization's AI practices and commitments:

  • Introduction Section: Core messaging about your AI trust commitment
  • Compliance Badges: Visual representation of certifications and standards
  • Company Description: Background information and core values
  • Privacy & Contact: Essential legal and contact information
Resources Tab

The Resources tab provides a centralized repository for AI governance documentation:

  • Document Management: Upload and organize policy documents
  • PDF Support: Secure document storage and download capabilities
  • Visibility Control: Manage which resources are publicly visible
  • Resource Organization: Categorize and describe document purposes
Subprocessors Tab

The Subprocessors tab ensures transparency about third-party AI service providers:

  • Vendor Management: Track AI service providers and their purposes
  • Location Tracking: Document where data processing occurs
  • Purpose Documentation: Clearly define each subprocessor's role
  • URL Management: Provide links to subprocessor information
Settings Tab

The Settings tab allows you to customize your AI Trust Center's appearance and visibility:

  • Appearance Customization: Logo, colors, and branding
  • Visibility Control: Enable or disable public access
  • Title Configuration: Customize the trust center title
  • Header Styling: Brand colors and visual identity

Overview Section Details

Introduction Components
  • Purpose of Trust Center: Explain your commitment to responsible AI use, data privacy, and ethical AI practices
  • Our Statement: Brief statement about your AI applications and their significance, including main objectives like data security and trust-building
  • Our Mission: Mission statement reflecting your values related to AI governance and ethics
Compliance and Certification Badges

The system supports the following compliance standards and certifications:

  • SOC2 Type I: Security, availability, and confidentiality controls
  • SOC2 Type II: Operational effectiveness of security controls
  • ISO 27001: Information security management systems
  • ISO 42001: AI management systems standard
  • CCPA: California Consumer Privacy Act compliance
  • GDPR: General Data Protection Regulation compliance
  • HIPAA: Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act
  • EU AI Act: European Union Artificial Intelligence Act
Company Description Components
  • Background: Explain your company, what you do, and why trust in AI is essential
  • Core Benefits: Key benefits like efficiency, security, customer support, and ethical AI practices
  • Compliance Documentation: Access to comprehensive compliance documentation and certifications
Privacy Policy and Contact Information
  • Terms of Service: Link to your terms of service document
  • Privacy Policy: Link to your privacy policy document
  • Company Email: Contact information for privacy and security-related questions

Resources Management

Resource Information Fields
  • Resource Name: Descriptive title for the document (required)
  • Type or Purpose: Description of the resource's purpose (required)
  • File Upload: PDF document upload (required, PDF format only)
  • Visibility: Control whether the resource is publicly visible
Resource Operations
  • Add Resources: Upload new policy documents and governance materials
  • Edit Resources: Update resource information and replace files
  • Delete Resources: Remove outdated or irrelevant documents
  • Download Resources: Provide secure download access to stakeholders
  • Visibility Toggle: Control public access to individual resources

Subprocessors Management

Subprocessor Information Fields
  • Company Name: Name of the subprocessor organization (required)
  • Purpose: Description of how the subprocessor is used (required)
  • URL: Link to the subprocessor's website or information (required)
  • Location: Geographic location where data processing occurs (required)
Subprocessor Operations
  • Add Subprocessors: Document new AI service providers
  • Edit Subprocessors: Update subprocessor information
  • Delete Subprocessors: Remove discontinued service providers
  • Transparency Display: Public visibility of subprocessor information

Settings and Customization

Appearance Settings
  • Company Logo: Upload and manage your organization's logo (PNG, JPG, GIF, SVG, max 5MB)
  • Header Color: Customize the header background color with color picker
  • Trust Center Title: Customize the title displayed on the public page
Visibility Settings
  • Enable AI Trust Center: Control public access to the trust center
  • Public URL: When enabled, accessible at /ai-trust-center directory
  • Preview Mode: Test how the trust center appears to external stakeholders

Preview Mode and Public Access

Preview Functionality

The Preview Mode button allows you to see exactly how your AI Trust Center appears to external stakeholders:

  • Public View: Opens the trust center in a new tab as it appears to visitors
  • Stakeholder Perspective: Experience the trust center from a customer or partner viewpoint
  • Content Verification: Ensure all information displays correctly and professionally
  • Access Testing: Verify that enabled sections and resources are properly accessible
Public Trust Center Features
  • Professional Layout: Clean, branded presentation of your AI governance information
  • Tabbed Navigation: Easy access to Overview, Resources, and Subprocessors
  • Download Capabilities: Secure document downloads for stakeholders
  • Responsive Design: Optimized viewing across different devices

Step-by-Step Trust Center Setup

Initial Configuration
  1. Navigate to the Settings tab to configure basic appearance
  2. Upload your company logo and set header colors
  3. Customize the trust center title
  4. Enable the AI Trust Center for public access
Content Creation
  1. Go to the Overview tab to create your introduction content
  2. Enable and fill in the Purpose, Statement, and Mission sections
  3. Select relevant compliance badges for your certifications
  4. Add company background and core benefits information
  5. Configure privacy policy and contact information
Resource Management
  1. Navigate to the Resources tab
  2. Enable the Resources section visibility
  3. Add policy documents, compliance reports, and governance materials
  4. Set appropriate visibility for each resource
  5. Test downloads to ensure proper functionality
Subprocessor Documentation
  1. Go to the Subprocessors tab
  2. Enable the Subprocessors section visibility
  3. Add all AI service providers and third-party vendors
  4. Document their purposes, locations, and contact information
  5. Ensure transparency and compliance with data processing requirements

Troubleshooting

  • Preview Issues: Ensure the AI Trust Center is enabled in Settings before using Preview Mode
  • File Upload Problems: Verify file format (PDF only for resources) and size limits (5MB for logos)
  • Visibility Issues: Check that individual sections are enabled before content appears publicly
  • Content Not Saving: Ensure all required fields are completed before saving
  • Public Access Problems: Verify that the AI Trust Center is enabled in Settings
  • Logo Display Issues: Check file format and size, ensure proper image formats are used
