AI Training Registry

The AI Training Registry serves as a comprehensive management system for all AI-related training programs within your organization. This centralized platform allows you to track, manage, and monitor training initiatives, ensuring your team stays current with AI governance best practices and compliance requirements.

System Overview

The Training Registry provides a complete solution for managing AI training programs, from initial planning through completion. It helps organizations maintain compliance with AI governance frameworks, track employee development, and ensure consistent training standards across departments.

Training Management Features

Training Creation and Management
  • New Training Creation: Add comprehensive training programs with detailed information
  • Training Editing: Update existing training details and status
  • Training Deletion: Remove outdated or cancelled training programs
  • Status Tracking: Monitor training progress through different lifecycle stages
Training Information Fields
  • Training Name: Descriptive title of the training program (required)
  • Duration: Length of the training program (required)
  • Provider: Organization or individual conducting the training (required)
  • Department: Organizational unit responsible for the training (required)
  • Status: Current state of the training program (required)
  • Number of People: Expected or actual number of participants (required, minimum 1)
  • Description: Detailed description of training content and objectives (optional)

Training Status Management

The system supports three distinct training statuses, each with specific color coding for easy visual identification:

  • Planned: Training programs that are scheduled but not yet started (Blue badge)
  • In Progress: Training programs currently being conducted (Yellow badge)
  • Completed: Training programs that have been finished (Green badge)

Training Table Structure

The training table displays all training programs in a comprehensive, sortable format with the following columns:

  • Training Name: The title of the training program
  • Duration: Length of the training program
  • Provider: Organization conducting the training
  • Department: Responsible organizational unit
  • Status: Current training status with color-coded badges
  • People: Number of participants
  • Actions: Edit and delete options (role-based access)

Step-by-Step Training Management

Adding a New Training Program
  1. Click the "New training" button in the top-right corner
  2. Fill in the required training information:
    • Enter a descriptive training name
    • Specify the training duration
    • Select or enter the training provider
    • Choose the responsible department
    • Set the initial status (typically "Planned")
    • Enter the expected number of participants
  3. Optionally add a detailed description of the training content
  4. Click "Save" to add the training to your registry
Editing Existing Training
  1. Click on any training row in the table to open the edit modal
  2. Modify the training details as needed
  3. Update the status to reflect current progress
  4. Click "Save" to update the training information
Deleting Training Programs
  1. Click the delete button (trash icon) in the Actions column
  2. Confirm the deletion in the warning dialog
  3. The training will be permanently removed from the registry

User Permissions and Access Control

The Training Registry implements role-based access control to ensure appropriate security and data integrity:

  • Admin Users: Full access to create, edit, and delete training programs
  • Editor Users: Limited access (typically view-only for training management)
  • Auditor Users: Read-only access to view training information

Note: The "New training" button will be disabled for users without appropriate permissions, and action buttons in the table will only be visible to authorized users.

Training Registry Benefits

  • Compliance Tracking: Maintain records of AI training for audit purposes
  • Resource Planning: Track training capacity and resource allocation
  • Progress Monitoring: Visual status tracking for training programs
  • Department Coordination: Organize training by organizational units
  • Provider Management: Track relationships with training providers
  • Audit Trail: Complete history of training program changes

Best Practices

  • Regular Updates: Keep training status current to maintain accurate records
  • Detailed Descriptions: Provide comprehensive training descriptions for better tracking
  • Accurate Participant Counts: Update participant numbers to reflect actual attendance
  • Status Management: Update training status promptly as programs progress
  • Provider Information: Maintain accurate provider details for future reference

Troubleshooting

  • Permission Issues: Contact your administrator if you cannot access training management features
  • Data Loading: Refresh the page if training data doesn't load properly
  • Form Validation: Ensure all required fields are completed before saving
  • Status Updates: Verify status changes are saved by checking the table display
