Manage and track all AI-related training programs and certifications in your organization.

Key Features

  • Track employee training progress and completion status
  • Manage training certifications and expiration dates
  • Schedule and organize training sessions
  • Generate training compliance reports

How to Use

  1. Add new training programs using the 'Add Training' button
  2. Assign training to team members
  3. Monitor completion status in the training table
  4. Download certificates and training records
