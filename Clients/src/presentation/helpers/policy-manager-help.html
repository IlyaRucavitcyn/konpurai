Policy Manager

The Policy Manager serves as a comprehensive governance platform for creating, managing, and maintaining AI policies within your organization. This centralized system ensures consistent policy implementation, compliance tracking, and effective policy lifecycle management across all AI initiatives.

System Overview

The Policy Manager provides a complete solution for AI policy governance, from initial creation through review, approval, and ongoing maintenance. It helps organizations establish clear AI governance frameworks, maintain compliance with regulatory requirements, and ensure consistent policy application across departments.

Policy Management Features

Policy Creation and Management
  • New Policy Creation: Create comprehensive AI policies with rich text content
  • Policy Editing: Update existing policies with version control
  • Policy Deletion: Remove outdated or superseded policies
  • Status Tracking: Monitor policy progress through approval workflow
Policy Information Fields
  • Title: Descriptive policy title (required, 1-64 characters)
  • Status: Current policy status in the approval workflow (required)
  • Tags: Categorization labels for policy organization (required, minimum 1 tag)
  • Next Review Date: Scheduled date for policy review (required)
  • Assigned Reviewers: Users responsible for policy review (required, minimum 1 reviewer)
  • Content: Rich text policy content with formatting options (optional)

Policy Status Management

The system supports five distinct policy statuses, each representing a different stage in the policy lifecycle:

  • Draft: Initial policy creation stage (Gray badge)
  • In Review: Policy under review by assigned reviewers (Orange badge)
  • Approved: Policy approved and ready for publication (Green badge)
  • Published: Policy officially published and active (Blue badge)
  • Archived: Policy no longer active but retained for reference (Gray badge)

Policy Dashboard Components

Status Overview Cards

The dashboard displays policy counts by status in color-coded cards, providing quick visual insights into your policy portfolio:

  • Real-time count of policies in each status
  • Color-coded status indicators for quick identification
  • Hover tooltips showing detailed status information
Policy Table Structure

The policy table displays all policies in a comprehensive, sortable format with the following columns:

  • Title: Policy title (truncated if over 30 characters)
  • Status: Current policy status with color-coded badges
  • Tags: Policy categorization tags (truncated if over 30 characters)
  • Next Review: Scheduled review date
  • Author: Policy creator
  • Last Updated: Most recent modification timestamp
  • Updated By: User who made the last update
  • Actions: Edit and delete options

Advanced Filtering and Search

Status Filtering

Filter policies by status using the dropdown selector:

  • All Policies: View all policies regardless of status
  • Draft: Show only draft policies
  • In Review: Show only policies under review
  • Approved: Show only approved policies
  • Published: Show only published policies
  • Archived: Show only archived policies
Search Functionality

Search policies by title using the expandable search bar:

  • Click the search icon to expand the search input
  • Type policy titles to find specific policies
  • Search is case-insensitive and matches partial titles
  • Search works in combination with status filtering

Rich Text Editor Features

The policy content editor provides comprehensive formatting options for creating professional policy documents:

  • Text Formatting: Bold, italic, and underline text styling
  • Heading Styles: H1, H2, and H3 heading options
  • Blockquotes: Highlight important policy sections
  • HTML Output: Content saved as structured HTML

Step-by-Step Policy Management

Creating a New Policy
  1. Click the "Add new policy" button in the top-right corner
  2. Fill in the required policy information:
    • Enter a descriptive policy title (1-64 characters)
    • Select initial status (typically "Draft")
    • Add at least one categorization tag
    • Set the next review date
    • Assign at least one reviewer
  3. Optionally add rich text content using the editor
  4. Click "Save" to create the policy
Editing Existing Policies
  1. Click on any policy row in the table to open the edit modal
  2. Modify the policy details as needed
  3. Update the status to reflect current progress
  4. Use the rich text editor to modify policy content
  5. Click "Save" to update the policy
Deleting Policies
  1. Click the delete button (trash icon) in the Actions column
  2. Confirm the deletion in the warning dialog
  3. The policy will be permanently removed from the system

User Management and Permissions

The Policy Manager integrates with your organization's user management system:

  • Author Assignment: Policies are automatically assigned to the creator
  • Reviewer Assignment: Assign specific users as policy reviewers
  • Update Tracking: Track who made the last update to each policy
  • User Lookup: Automatic user name resolution from user IDs

Troubleshooting

  • Form Validation: Ensure all required fields are completed before saving
  • Tag Requirements: Add at least one tag to categorize the policy
  • Reviewer Assignment: Assign at least one reviewer before saving
  • Date Format: Use the date picker for proper date formatting
  • Content Saving: Verify rich text content is properly formatted
  • Status Updates: Confirm status changes are saved by checking the table
